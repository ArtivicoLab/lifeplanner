Privacy Policy

Life Planner, by ArtivicoLab · Last updated: July 6, 2026

Life Planner is a personal planning app that runs entirely in your web browser. This policy explains what data the app handles and, importantly, what it does not do. The short version: we run no server, we never receive your data, and your information lives on your own device and in your own Google account.

Who we are

Life Planner is created and operated by ArtivicoLab. You can reach us any time at artivicolab@gmail.com.

What data the app handles

  • Your planner content (tasks, habits, budgets, meals, and so on) is stored locally on your device in your browser (IndexedDB).
  • If you choose to connect Google, that same content is also saved to a single spreadsheet the app creates in your own Google Drive, so it syncs across your devices and you have a backup you own.
  • We do not operate any server or database of our own, so your planner content is never sent to us or to any third party.

Google user data

When you connect Google, the app requests the minimum access it needs:

  • Google Sheets / Drive (drive.file scope): access is limited to the single spreadsheet Life Planner creates for you. The app cannot see or touch any of your other Drive files.

Your Google sign-in token is stored only in your browser and is used only to talk to Google's APIs directly from your device. We never see, receive, or store it.

Limited Use disclosure: Life Planner's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. We do not use Google user data for advertising, and we do not sell it or transfer it to others.

How your data is used

Solely to provide the planner: to show, save, and sync the content you enter. That is it. We do not use it for advertising, analytics profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the app.

Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data. It never leaves your device except to your own Google account, and only when you choose to connect.

Keeping and deleting your data

  • Disconnect Google any time in the app's Settings.
  • Delete the spreadsheet from your own Google Drive to remove the synced copy.
  • Clear the app's data on a device (Settings, or your browser's site data) to remove the local copy.

Children

Life Planner is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we will change the date at the top of this page.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email artivicolab@gmail.com.

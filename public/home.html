Life Planner

by ArtivicoLab

An all-in-one personal planner that lives in your own Google Sheet.

Life Planner is a phone-first web app that helps you organize your whole life in one place. It opens in any browser on your phone, tablet, or computer, works offline, and can sync your data to a spreadsheet in your own Google Drive so you always own a backup.

Open Life Planner

What you can do

  • Plan one-off and recurring tasks with priorities, and see them on a live dashboard
  • Track habits and streaks, set goals with step checklists, and block your time
  • Manage a budget with income, bills, expenses, savings, and debt payoff
  • Plan meals, auto-build a grocery list, and log fitness, weight, and hydration
  • See everything on a month, week, and day calendar that fills every date

How Life Planner uses your Google account

Connecting Google is optional. When you choose to connect, Life Planner requests only the Google Drive "drive.file" permission. This limits access to the single spreadsheet the app creates for you, and nothing else in your Drive.

We use that access for one purpose: to save and sync your planner to that spreadsheet so it is backed up and available across your devices. Your sign-in stays in your browser and talks to Google directly from your device. ArtivicoLab operates no server and never receives, stores, or sells your data. See our Privacy Policy for the full details.

Your data stays yours

Everything works offline on your device first. If you connect Google, your data lives in a spreadsheet in your own Google Drive that you can open, export, or delete any time. There is no subscription, and connecting to Google Sheets is free.
